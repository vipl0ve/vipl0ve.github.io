ABOUT ME

I have a passion for solving problems through entrepreneurial innovative solutions, and I utilize my engineering background, technical expertise, and experience in software development and product management to build a finalize product.

Prior to MBA, I have worked on all stages of software development lifecycle: as a business analyst, gather clients requirements, and as a product manager, defining new features from those requirements, as a software developer, coding those product features, and as a quality assurance, verifying and validating those features. I also built great relationships with the clients and team members. Furthermore, I led the Agile planning activities, including the scrums and sprint plannings.

Post MBA I want to focus on understanding clients requirements and build new features and business strategies to provide great clients experience and higher client adoption. Furthermore, I want to be a great intermediatory between business and technology team.

I believe the MBA program has helped me in enhancing my business acumen, provided me a deep understanding of various product and marketing concepts and improved my soft skills.
